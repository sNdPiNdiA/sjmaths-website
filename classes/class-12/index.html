Frequently Asked Questions

How to prepare for Class 12 Maths Board Exam?

Focus on mastering NCERT concepts first. Then, practice Previous Year Questions (PYQs) and solve sample papers to understand the exam pattern and manage time effectively.

Is NCERT enough for Class 12 Maths Board Exam?

NCERT textbooks cover the entire syllabus and are sufficient for understanding concepts. However, for scoring above 90%, practicing NCERT Exemplar problems and past year papers is highly recommended.

Where can I download Class 12 Maths Chapter-wise Notes?
