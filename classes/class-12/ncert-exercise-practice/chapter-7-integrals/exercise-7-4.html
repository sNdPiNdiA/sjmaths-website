$\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{(3x)^2+2^2}}$. Let $3x = t \Rightarrow 3dx = dt$.

$\boxed{\frac{1}{3}\log|3x+\sqrt{9x^2+4}| + C}$

$\boxed{\sqrt{x^2-9} + \log|x+\sqrt{x^2-9}| + C}$
