Chapter 5: Arithmetic Progressions

MCQ Worksheet

Back to Worksheets
  1. Question 1: The 10th term of the AP: 2, 7, 12, ... is:
    (A) 47
    (B) 50
    (C) 52
    (D) 57
    Solution: (A) 47
    Step 1: Here $a=2, d=7-2=5$.
    Step 2: $a_{10} = a + (10-1)d = 2 + 9(5) = 2 + 45 = 47$.
  2. Question 2: Which term of the AP: 21, 18, 15, ... is -81?
    (A) 34
    (B) 35
    (C) 36
    (D) 33
    Solution: (B) 35
    Step 1: $a=21, d=-3, a_n=-81$.
    Step 2: $-81 = 21 + (n-1)(-3) \Rightarrow -102 = -3(n-1) \Rightarrow 34 = n-1 \Rightarrow n=35$.
  3. Question 3: The sum of the first five multiples of 3 is:
    (A) 45
    (B) 55
    (C) 65
    (D) 75
    Solution: (A) 45
    Step 1: AP is 3, 6, 9, 12, 15.
    Step 2: Sum $= 3+6+9+12+15 = 45$.
  4. Question 4: If $k, 2k-1$ and $2k+1$ are three consecutive terms of an AP, the value of $k$ is:
    (A) 2
    (B) 3
    (C) -3
    (D) 6
    Solution: (B) 3
    Step 1: For AP, $2b = a+c$. So, $2(2k-1) = k + (2k+1)$.
    Step 2: $4k - 2 = 3k + 1 \Rightarrow k = 3$.
  5. Question 5: The common difference of the AP $\frac{1}{p}, \frac{1-p}{p}, \frac{1-2p}{p}, \dots$ is:
    (A) $p$
    (B) $-p$
    (C) $-1$
    (D) $1$
    Solution: (C) -1
    Step 1: $d = a_2 - a_1 = \frac{1-p}{p} - \frac{1}{p} = \frac{1-p-1}{p} = \frac{-p}{p} = -1$.
  6. Question 6: If the $n^{th}$ term of an AP is $3n + 4$, then the common difference is:
    (A) 7
    (B) 3
    (C) 4
    (D) 1
    Solution: (B) 3
    Step 1: $a_1 = 3(1)+4 = 7$, $a_2 = 3(2)+4 = 10$.
    Step 2: $d = 10 - 7 = 3$. (Coefficient of $n$ is always $d$).
  7. Question 7: The sum of first $n$ terms of an AP is $3n^2 + n$. Then the second term of this AP is:
    (A) 14
    (B) 10
    (C) 4
    (D) 7
    Solution: (B) 10
    Step 1: $S_1 = a_1 = 3(1)^2 + 1 = 4$.
    Step 2: $S_2 = a_1 + a_2 = 3(2)^2 + 2 = 14$.
    Step 3: $a_2 = S_2 - S_1 = 14 - 4 = 10$.
  8. Question 8: The sum of first 20 odd natural numbers is:
    (A) 100
    (B) 210
    (C) 400
    (D) 420
    Solution: (C) 400
    Step 1: Sum of first $n$ odd numbers is $n^2$.
    Step 2: Here $n=20$, so Sum $= 20^2 = 400$.
  9. Question 9: If the 7th term of an AP is 4 and its common difference is -4, then its first term is:
    (A) 16
    (B) 20
    (C) 24
    (D) 28
    Solution: (D) 28
    Step 1: $a_7 = a + 6d = 4$.
    Step 2: $a + 6(-4) = 4 \Rightarrow a - 24 = 4 \Rightarrow a = 28$.
  10. Question 10: The 4th term from the end of the AP: -11, -8, ..., 49 is:
    (A) 37
    (B) 40
    (C) 43
    (D) 58
    Solution: (B) 40
    Step 1: Reverse the AP: 49, 46, ... ($d = -3$).
    Step 2: 4th term $= a + 3d = 49 + 3(-3) = 49 - 9 = 40$.
