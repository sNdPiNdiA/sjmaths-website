  1. Case Study 1: Health Checkup Camp

    A medical camp was held in a school to impart health education and the importance of exercise to children. During this camp, a medical check of 35 students was done and their weights were recorded as follows:

    Weight (in kg)Less than 38Less than 40Less than 42Less than 44Less than 46Less than 48Less than 50Less than 52
    No. of Students035914283235
    1. Find the median weight of the students.
      (A) 46.5 kg
      (B) 47.5 kg
      (C) 45.5 kg
      (D) 46 kg
      Solution: (A) 46.5 kg
      Step 1: Convert to frequency distribution. Classes: 38-40 (3), 40-42 (2), 42-44 (4), 44-46 (5), 46-48 (14), 48-50 (4), 50-52 (3).
      Step 2: $N=35, N/2=17.5$. CF just greater than 17.5 is 28. Median class is 46-48.
      Step 3: $l=46, h=2, f=14, cf=14$. Median $= 46 + \frac{17.5 - 14}{14} \times 2 = 46 + \frac{3.5}{7} = 46 + 0.5 = 46.5$ kg.
    2. Find the modal weight of the students.
      (A) 46.9 kg
      (B) 47.2 kg
      (C) 46.95 kg
      (D) 47.5 kg
      Solution: (C) 46.95 kg
      Step 1: Max frequency is 14. Modal class is 46-48.
      Step 2: $l=46, f_1=14, f_0=5, f_2=4, h=2$.
      Step 3: Mode $= 46 + \frac{14-5}{2(14)-5-4} \times 2 = 46 + \frac{9}{19} \times 2 = 46 + \frac{18}{19} \approx 46.95$ kg.
  2. Case Study 2: Mileage of Cars

    The mileage (km per litre) of 50 cars of the same model was tested by a manufacturer and details are tabulated as given below:

    Mileage (km/l)10-1212-1414-1616-18
    No. of Cars7121813
    1. Find the mean mileage of the cars.
      (A) 14.48 km/l
      (B) 14.52 km/l
      (C) 15.12 km/l
      (D) 13.98 km/l
      Solution: (A) 14.48 km/l
      Step 1: $x_i$: 11, 13, 15, 17. $f_i$: 7, 12, 18, 13.
      Step 2: $f_i x_i$: 77, 156, 270, 221. $\sum f_i x_i = 724$. $\sum f_i = 50$.
      Step 3: Mean $= 724/50 = 14.48$ km/l.
    2. Find the median mileage.
      (A) 14.5 km/l
      (B) 14.67 km/l
      (C) 14.8 km/l
      (D) 15.2 km/l
      Solution: (B) 14.67 km/l
      Step 1: $N=50, N/2=25$. CF: 7, 19, 37, 50. Median class 14-16.
      Step 2: $l=14, cf=19, f=18, h=2$.
      Step 3: Median $= 14 + \frac{25-19}{18} \times 2 = 14 + \frac{6}{9} = 14 + 0.67 = 14.67$ km/l.
