Example: Find the distance between points $A(2, 3)$ and $B(4, 1)$.
Formula: $d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}$
Substitution: $\sqrt{(4-2)^2 + (1-3)^2}$
Final Answer: $2\sqrt{2}$ units
