Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to prepare for Class 10 Maths board exams?

Start with NCERT textbooks, use our chapter notes for revision, and solve previous year questions (PYQs) to understand the exam pattern.

Are the Class 10 Maths notes and solutions free?

Yes, all resources on SJMaths, including notes, NCERT solutions, and practice tests, are completely free for students.

Does SJMaths provide solutions for standard and basic maths?

Our materials cover the complete NCERT syllabus, making them suitable for both Standard and Basic Mathematics students.
