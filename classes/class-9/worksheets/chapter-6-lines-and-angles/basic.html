Chapter 6: Lines and Angles

Basic Level Worksheet

Back to Worksheets
  1. Question 1: What is the measure of a straight angle?
    Solution: The measure of a straight angle is $180^\circ$.
  2. Question 2: If two lines intersect each other, then the vertically opposite angles are?
    Solution: They are equal.
  3. Question 3: Find the complement of $65^\circ$.
    Solution: Complement = $90^\circ - 65^\circ = 25^\circ$.
  4. Question 4: If one angle of a linear pair is acute, then the other angle is?
    Solution: The other angle must be obtuse.
  5. Question 5: What is the sum of the angles of a triangle?
    Solution: The sum is $180^\circ$.
  6. Question 6: In a linear pair, if one angle is $90^\circ$, what is the other?
    Solution: The other angle is $180^\circ - 90^\circ = 90^\circ$.
  7. Question 7: Name the angle which is equal to its supplement.
    Solution: Let the angle be $x$. Then $x + x = 180^\circ \Rightarrow 2x = 180^\circ \Rightarrow x = 90^\circ$.
  8. Question 8: If a transversal intersects two parallel lines, then alternate interior angles are?
    Solution: They are equal.
  9. Question 9: An angle is greater than $90^\circ$ and less than $180^\circ$. What type of angle is it?
    Solution: It is an Obtuse angle.
  10. Question 10: What is the sum of all angles around a point?
    Solution: The sum is $360^\circ$.
