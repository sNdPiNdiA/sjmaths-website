Chapter 7: Triangles

Basic Level Worksheet

Back to Worksheets
  1. Question 1: How many vertices does a triangle have?
    Solution: A triangle has 3 vertices.
  2. Question 2: What is the sum of the angles in a triangle?
    Solution: The sum of the angles in a triangle is $180^\circ$.
  3. Question 3: A triangle with all three sides equal is called?
    Solution: An equilateral triangle.
  4. Question 4: A triangle with two sides equal is called?
    Solution: An isosceles triangle.
  5. Question 5: A triangle with all three sides of different lengths is called?
    Solution: A scalene triangle.
  6. Question 6: Can a triangle have two right angles?
    Solution: No, because the sum of two right angles is $180^\circ$, which means the third angle would be $0^\circ$.
  7. Question 7: What is the side opposite to the right angle in a right-angled triangle called?
    Solution: It is called the hypotenuse.
  8. Question 8: If two angles of a triangle are $60^\circ$ and $70^\circ$, what is the third angle?
    Solution: The third angle is $180^\circ - (60^\circ + 70^\circ) = 50^\circ$.
  9. Question 9: What does SSS congruence criterion stand for?
    Solution: Side-Side-Side congruence criterion.
  10. Question 10: What does SAS congruence criterion stand for?
    Solution: Side-Angle-Side congruence criterion.
