Chapter 8: Quadrilaterals

Basic Level Worksheet

Back to Worksheets
  1. Question 1: How many sides does a quadrilateral have?
    Solution: A quadrilateral has 4 sides.
  2. Question 2: What is the sum of the angles in a quadrilateral?
    Solution: The sum of the angles in a quadrilateral is $360^\circ$.
  3. Question 3: A quadrilateral with both pairs of opposite sides parallel is called a?
    Solution: Parallelogram.
  4. Question 4: What is a parallelogram with all four sides equal called?
    Solution: A rhombus.
  5. Question 5: What is a parallelogram with all four angles equal to $90^\circ$ called?
    Solution: A rectangle.
  6. Question 6: A rectangle with all four sides equal is called a?
    Solution: A square.
  7. Question 7: In a parallelogram, opposite sides are ________.
    Solution: Equal and parallel.
  8. Question 8: In a parallelogram, opposite angles are ________.
    Solution: Equal.
  9. Question 9: The diagonals of a parallelogram ________ each other.
    Solution: Bisect.
  10. Question 10: Three angles of a quadrilateral are $75^\circ, 90^\circ$ and $75^\circ$. Find the fourth angle.
    Solution: The fourth angle is $360^\circ - (75^\circ + 90^\circ + 75^\circ) = 360^\circ - 240^\circ = 120^\circ$.
